---
title: "Choosing durability"
---

EDB Postgres Distributed allows you to choose from several repliction configurations based on your consistency, availability, and performance needs:

* Asynchronous
* Synchronous
* [Commit at Most Once](/bdr/latest/commit_at_most_once.mdx)
* [Eager](/bdr/latest/eager_replication.mdx)
* [Group Commit](/bdr/latest/group_commit.mdx)


For more information, see [Durability](/bdr/latest/durability.mdx).
