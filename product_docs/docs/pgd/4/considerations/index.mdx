In addition to choosing the archiitecure that best meets your needs, the following choices are critical for choosing the correct configuration when deploying:

* Replication method (Async, Sync, Eager Replication, Commit at Most Once, or Group Commit)
* Database Distribution (PostreSQL, EDB Postgres Extended Server, or EDB Postgres Advanced Server)
