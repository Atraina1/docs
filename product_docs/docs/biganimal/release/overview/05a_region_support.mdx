## Supported regions

 When using Azure, you can create a cluster in the following regions:

- (Asia Pacific) Australia East
- (Asia Pacific) Japan East
- (Asia Pacific) South East Asia
- (Canada) Canada Central
- (Europe) France Central
- (Europe) North Europe
- (Europe) Norway East
- (Europe) UK South
- (Europe) West Europe
- (South America) Brazil South
- (US) Central US
- (US) East US
- (US) East US 2
- (US) South Central US
- (US) West US 2
- (US) West US 3
